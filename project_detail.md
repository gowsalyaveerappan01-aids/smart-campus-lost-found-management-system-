# Smart Campus Lost & Found Management System

## Project Title

**Smart Campus Lost & Found Management System**

## Problem Statement

In educational institutions, students and staff often lose personal belongings within the campus. Traditional methods of reporting and tracking lost items are inefficient, time-consuming, and lack proper record management. Therefore, there is a need for a centralized system to manage lost and found items effectively.

## Project Objectives

* To provide a platform for reporting lost and found items.
* To maintain a centralized database of lost and found records.
* To enable users to search and track reported items easily.
* To improve the efficiency of recovering lost belongings.
* To reduce manual efforts in managing lost and found activities within the campus.
